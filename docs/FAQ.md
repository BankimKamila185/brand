# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - The Outliers Studio

Get quick answers to the most common questions about orders, streetwear sizing, shipping, payments, and returns.

---

## 1. Sizing & Product Information

### Q: What does "Oversized Fit" mean?
A: Our oversized t-shirts, hoodies, and sweatshirts are designed to have a relaxed, baggy drape with dropped shoulders. They run larger than traditional standard-fit apparel. We recommend purchasing your normal size for the intended oversized look. If you prefer a regular, snugger fit, consider sizing down.

### Q: Where can I find a Size Chart?
A: A detailed [Size Guide](Customer-Support.md#size-guide) is available on every product page. Please measure yourself before purchasing to avoid exchange delays.

### Q: How do I care for my graphic streetwear prints?
A: To preserve the prints and heavy-weight fabric, we recommend washing inside out in cold water, using a mild detergent. Do not iron directly on prints, and air dry in shade. See our complete [Wash Care Guide](Customer-Support.md#wash-care-guide) for details.

## 2. Shipping & Delivery

### Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Orders are processed within 24–48 hours. Delivery timelines range from **3 to 7 business days** across India, depending on your location (metro cities are usually faster). Check our [Shipping Policy](Shipping-Policy.md) for more details.

### Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, we will email and SMS you a tracking link. You can also track your shipment directly using our [Track Order](Customer-Support.md#track-order) link.

## 3. Payments & Security

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: Through our secure **Razorpay Payment Gateway**, we accept:
* All major Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Diners Club)
* Unified Payments Interface (UPI) (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
* Net Banking (all major Indian banks)
* Selected Net-Banking and Digital Wallets

### Q: Is my payment secure?
A: Yes. All transactions are processed through Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant secure servers using 256-bit SSL encryption. We do not store your credit card or payment credentials.

---

## 4. Cancellations, Returns & Exchanges

### Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, you can cancel your order within **2 hours** of placing it, or before it is shipped (whichever is earlier). Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled. Read our [Cancellation Policy](Cancellation-Policy.md) for details.

### Q: What is your Return Policy?
A: We offer a **7-day return policy** from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, with all original tags and packaging intact. Accessories and custom drops may be excluded. Review our [Return Policy](Return-Policy.md).

### Q: How long do refunds take?
A: Once your return is received and inspected at our warehouse, we will approve or reject your refund. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment source via Razorpay within **5 to 7 business days**. For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds are processed via UPI or Bank Transfer within 7 business days. Read our [Refund Policy](Refund-Policy.md).
