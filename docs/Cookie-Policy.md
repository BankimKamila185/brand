## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and assist in marketing personalization.
* **First-party cookies**: Set by the website you are visiting (e.g. cookies set by `[WEBSITE_URL]`).
* **Third-party cookies**: Set by a domain other than the website you are visiting (e.g. cookies set by Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or Razorpay).

## 2. Categories of Cookies We Use
We categorize cookies into five types based on their purpose:

### A. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Essential)
These cookies are critical for the basic operations of the website. Without these, the website cannot function properly.
* **Purpose**: Managing user sessions, maintaining shopping cart contents, handling checkout processes, and ensuring security (such as CSRF protection).
* **Consent**: These cookies are enabled by default and cannot be opted out of, as the website cannot run without them.
* **Examples**: Shopping cart status, login credentials token, CSRF token, Razorpay checkout session cookies.

### B. Performance & Analytics Cookies
These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors interact with our website.
* **Purpose**: Helping us understand which pages are visited most, how users navigate, and if they encounter errors. We use this data to improve website speed and layout.
* **Consent**: Optional. These are blocked by default until you provide explicit consent via our Cookie Banner.
* **Examples**: Google Analytics tracking cookies (`_ga`, `_gid`), Google Tag Manager integration tags.

### C. Marketing & Targeting Cookies
These cookies track your browsing habits across different websites to deliver advertisements that are more relevant to your interests.
* **Purpose**: Showing you targeted ads for our premium streetwear on social media platforms, measuring the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns, and preventing the same ad from showing repeatedly.
* **Consent**: Optional. These are blocked by default until you provide explicit consent.
* **Examples**: Meta Pixel tracking cookies (`_fbp`), Google Ads remarketing tags.

### D. Functional Cookies
These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
* **Purpose**: Remembering choices you make (such as UI theme settings or custom layouts).
* **Consent**: Optional. If disabled, some interactive features may not function correctly.

### E. Preference Cookies
These cookies store your preferences to customize your browsing experience.
* **Purpose**: Remembering your selected language, region, or currency (INR).
* **Consent**: Optional.

## 3. Third-Party Tracking Technologies Used
To support our premium e-commerce infrastructure, we integrate with several reputable third-party tools that place cookies:

## 4. How to Manage and Disable Cookies
You have full control over the optional cookies we place on your device:

* **Cookie Consent Banner**: When you first visit our Website, a banner appears letting you accept all cookies, decline optional cookies, or open "Settings" to customize your categories.
* **Browser Settings**: You can configure your browser to block all cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. 
  * Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Third-party cookies
  * Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Block all cookies
  * Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
* *Note: If you block all cookies (including strictly necessary ones), you will not be able to log in to your account, add items to your cart, or complete a checkout on our store.*
