# Shipping & Delivery Policy - The Outliers Studio

*Last Updated: July 20, 2026*

At **The Outliers Studio**, we want to make sure your streetwear drops reach you as fast and securely as possible. This Shipping & Delivery Policy describes our processing times, delivery zones, shipping fees, and tracking procedures across India.

---

## 1. Shipping Rates & Free Shipping Thresholds
* **Prepaid Orders**: 
  * We offer **Free Shipping** across India on all prepaid orders exceeding **INR 999**.
  * For prepaid orders under INR 999, a nominal flat shipping fee of **INR 99** is charged.
* **Cash on Delivery (COD) Orders**:
  * If COD is available, a flat handling fee of **INR 149** applies to all COD orders, regardless of the order value. This fee covers extra validation and delivery collection costs.

---

## 2. Delivery Timelines & Zones
We partner with leading domestic courier services (including Blue Dart, Delhivery, Xpressbees, and Shadowfax) to ensure reliable delivery.

| Location Category | Regions Covered | Estimated Delivery Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Metros & Tier 1** | Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi-NCR, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad | **3 to 5 Business Days** |
| **Tier 2 & Tier 3** | Other state capitals, major district towns, and urban centers | **5 to 7 Business Days** |
| **Rest of India** | Remote areas, Northeast states, Jammu & Kashmir, and island territories | **7 to 10 Business Days** |

*Please Note: These timelines are estimates. Deliveries may occasionally be delayed due to bad weather, public holidays, regional restrictions, or courier operational issues. We will inform you via email/SMS if there is a known delay.*

---

## 3. Order Processing & Dispatch
* **Dispatch Timeline**: Orders are processed and dispatched from our warehouse in Bengaluru within **24 to 48 hours** (excluding Sundays and national holidays) of order placement.
* **Order Cut-Off**: Orders placed before 12:00 PM (IST) are typically processed for dispatch on the same day.

---

## 4. Tracking Your Shipment
* **Notification**: As soon as your order is handed over to our courier partner, we will send an email and SMS containing your unique tracking number and the tracking link.
* **Real-Time Tracking**: You can monitor your shipment status using our [Track Order](Customer-Support.md#track-order) portal or directly on the courier partner's tracking website. It may take up to 24 hours for the tracking status to become active online after dispatch.

---

## 5. Delivery Attempts & Unclaimed Shipments
* **Attempts**: Our logistics partners will make up to **three (3) delivery attempts** to deliver the package.
* **Contact**: The courier agent will attempt to call you on the mobile number provided during checkout.
* **Return to Origin (RTO)**: If you are unavailable or the address is incorrect/incomplete after 3 attempts, the shipment will be returned to our warehouse as RTO. 
  * For prepaid RTO orders, our team will contact you to arrange redelivery (a redelivery charge of INR 99 may apply) or issue a refund minus the initial shipping charges.
  * For COD RTO orders, your COD privilege may be suspended for future orders on our store.

---

## 7. Damaged or Lost Shipments
* **Damaged Packaging**: If you receive a package that is visibly damaged, tampered with, or open, **please do not accept it**. Take photos/videos of the package and immediately contact us at `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]`.
* **Lost Shipments**: If a package is declared lost by our courier partner, we will either dispatch a replacement product (subject to stock availability) or issue a full refund to your original payment source.
