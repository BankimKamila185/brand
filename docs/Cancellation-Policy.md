# Cancellation Policy - The Outliers Studio

*Last Updated: July 20, 2026*

This Cancellation Policy outlines how you can cancel an order placed with **The Outliers Studio** and the terms that apply to cancellations.

---

## 1. Cancellation Window
We process and ship orders quickly to ensure rapid delivery. You can cancel your order only within the following timelines:
* **Pre-Dispatch**: You may cancel your order within **2 hours** of placing it, or before it is dispatched from our warehouse, whichever is earlier.
* **Post-Dispatch**: Once your order has been handed over to the courier partner and marked as shipped, it **cannot be cancelled**.

---

## 2. How to Request a Cancellation
* **Via Your Account (Fastest)**: 
  1. Log in to your account at `[WEBSITE_URL]`.
  2. Navigate to "My Account" > "My Orders".
  3. Select the order and click the "Cancel Order" button (this button will only be visible during the eligible pre-dispatch window).
* **Via Email**: If you checked out as a guest, please email `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]` immediately. 
  * The email subject must be: **"URGENT: Order Cancellation - [Your Order ID]"**.
  * Please state the reason for cancellation. 
  * *Note: Email requests are processed during working hours. If your order is dispatched before our support team reviews your email, the cancellation cannot be completed.*

---

## 3. Refunds for Cancelled Orders
* **Prepaid Orders**: If you cancel an order that was paid online, we will initiate a full refund of the order value (including shipping fees, if any) via Razorpay back to your original payment source.
* **Timeline**: The refund is initiated immediately upon cancellation. The funds will reflect in your account within **5 to 7 business days** (for card/bank payments) or **2 to 3 business days** (for UPI payments).
* **COD Orders**: No refund is required for COD cancellations as no payment was collected.

---

## 4. Non-Cancellable Orders
Cancellations are not permitted under the following conditions:
* **Post-Dispatch**: Once the shipment is in transit.
* **Custom/Personalized Goods**: Any product customized or tailored specifically for you.
* **Special Event Drops**: Certain limited-edition drops or flash sales where the product page explicitly states "No Cancellations".

---

## 5. Doorstep Rejection (Refusal of Delivery)
If you refuse to accept delivery of a prepaid order at your doorstep:
* The package will be returned to our warehouse as a Return to Origin (RTO).
* Once received, we will refund the order amount, but we will deduct a flat RTO handling and shipping charge of **INR 99** from your refund amount.
* Repeated doorstep rejections of COD orders will lead to the suspension of your Cash on Delivery privilege.
