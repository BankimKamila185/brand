# Cookie Consent Banner & Settings Copy - The Outliers Studio

This document contains production-ready copy for the Cookie Consent Banner and the Cookie Preferences Modal on **The Outliers Studio** website.

---

## 1. Main Cookie Banner

### UI Placement
A bottom overlay banner appearing when a user first lands on the website. The user must either accept all, decline optional, or open preferences.

### Copy
* **Heading**: "We respect your style & privacy."
* **Body Text**: 
  > "We use cookies to enhance your shopping experience, display personalized streetwear recommendations, and analyze our traffic. By clicking 'Accept All', you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with our [Cookie Policy](Cookie-Policy.md). You can customize your choices under 'Settings'."
* **Action Buttons**:
  * `[Accept All]` (Primary color button - e.g. solid black)
  * `[Decline Optional]` (Outline secondary button)
  * `[Cookie Settings]` (Text link / minimal button)

---

## 2. Cookie Settings Preferences Modal

### UI Placement
Triggered when the user clicks `[Cookie Settings]` on the main banner or the "Manage Cookies" link in the footer.

* **Modal Title**: "Cookie Preferences"
* **Modal Subtitle**: 
  > "Choose which categories of cookies you consent to. Strictly necessary cookies are required to manage your shopping session and cannot be disabled. For details, read our [Cookie Policy](Cookie-Policy.md)."

### Categories & Descriptions

#### 1. Strictly Necessary Cookies (Mandatory)
* **Toggle State**: Checked by default and Disabled (User cannot uncheck).
* **Copy**:
  > "**Strictly Necessary Cookies (Always Active)**: These cookies are essential for you to navigate our store, maintain your shopping session, add products to your cart, and complete secure payments via Razorpay. Without these cookies, the website will not work."

#### 2. Performance & Analytics Cookies (Optional)
* **Toggle State**: Unchecked by default (User must toggle on to accept).
* **Copy**:
  > "**Performance & Analytics Cookies**: These cookies allow us to count visits, track traffic sources, and identify which product pages are most popular. This helps us optimize site performance, reduce loading times, and improve navigation. All data is aggregated and kept anonymous."

#### 3. Marketing & Targeting Cookies (Optional)
* **Toggle State**: Unchecked by default (User must toggle on to accept).
* **Copy**:
  > "**Marketing & Targeting Cookies**: These cookies are set through our website by advertising partners (such as Meta Pixel and Google Ads). They are used to build a profile of your fashion interests and display relevant apparel advertisements to you on other websites and social platforms."

#### 4. Functional Cookies (Optional)
* **Toggle State**: Unchecked by default.
* **Copy**:
  > "**Functional Cookies**: These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced personalization and features (such as retaining items in your shopping cart for your next visit, or custom user interface themes)."

#### 5. Preference Cookies (Optional)
* **Toggle State**: Unchecked by default.
* **Copy**:
  > "**Preference Cookies**: These cookies are used to remember your preferred settings, such as language preferences or region selection, to ensure a customized browsing experience."

### Footer Buttons (Modal)
* `[Save My Preferences]` (Saves the user's specific toggles)
* `[Allow All]` (Enables all categories and closes modal)
