# Checkout Page Legal Content & Disclaimers - The Outliers Studio

This document contains production-ready copy for checkboxes, legal notices, and product disclaimers displayed on the Checkout page of **The Outliers Studio**.

---

## 1. Mandatory Checkboxes & Consent

### A. Terms and Conditions & Privacy Policy Agreement
* **UI Placement**: Positioned directly above the final "Pay Now" button.
* **Checkbox State**: Unchecked by default. Must be ticked by the user to proceed.
* **Copy**:
  > "I have read, understood, and agree to the [Terms & Conditions](Terms-and-Conditions.md) and [Privacy Policy](Privacy-Policy.md) of The Outliers Studio. I consent to the processing of my personal data for order fulfillment."

### B. Shipping, Return, and Refund Policy Acceptance
* **UI Placement**: Positioned next to the shipping details form.
* **Checkbox State**: Unchecked by default.
* **Copy**:
  > "I acknowledge that I have reviewed and agree to the [Shipping Policy](Shipping-Policy.md) (estimated 3–7 business days delivery) and [Return & Refund Policy](Return-Policy.md) (7-day returns for eligible unused items, reverse pickups subject to location availability)."
