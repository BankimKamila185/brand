## 3. How to Submit a Grievance
To ensure a speedy resolution, your written complaint must contain:
1. **Order Details**: Your Order ID, Transaction ID, and registered phone number/email address.
2. **Detailed Description**: A clear summary of the issue (e.g. refund pending, delivery exception, incorrect item received, or privacy concern).
3. **Evidence**: Relevant attachments, such as screenshots of payment deductions, chat histories, or product photographs.
4. **Resolution Sought**: What specific action you would like us to take to resolve the issue.

---

## 4. Timelines & SLA for Resolution
Our Grievance Redressal Officer operates under the following legal timelines:
* **Acknowledgment**: We will send you an email or SMS acknowledging receipt of your grievance within **36 to 48 hours** of filing. This message will include a unique **Grievance Ticket Number** for tracking.
* **Resolution**: We will investigate and resolve your grievance within **30 days** (one month) from the date of receipt.
