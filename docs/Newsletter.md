This document contains copy templates for our customer newsletter subscription confirmation, unsubscribe page flows, and standard email newsletter templates.

## 2. Unsubscribe Confirmation Page Copy

### UI Placement
When a customer clicks the "Unsubscribe" link in the footer of our marketing emails, they are directed to this landing page on `[WEBSITE_URL]/unsubscribe`.

* **Heading**: "We're sorry to see you go."
* **Subheading**: "Your email (`user@example.com`) has been successfully unsubscribed from our marketing mailing lists."
* **Supporting Text**:
  * > "Please note that it may take up to 24–48 hours for our email databases to fully sync. You will no longer receive drops, launches, or discount updates from us."
  * > "If you placed an order, please be assured that you will still receive critical **transactional emails** regarding your purchases (such as order confirmations, tracking details, and refunds)."
* **Feedback Questionnaire (Optional)**:
  * "Help us improve. Why are you leaving? (Select all that apply)"
    * `[ ]` The emails were too frequent.
    * `[ ]` The content was not relevant to my style.
    * `[ ]` I purchased what I needed.
    * `[ ]` I subscribed by accident.
* **Resubscribe Action**:
  * "Made a mistake? [Click here to Resubscribe](file:///resubscribe-link)."

## 3. Regular Newsletter Template Structure
Every marketing newsletter we send out using Resend must include a clear, compliant footer containing:
1. **Unsubscribe Link**: A direct, single-click opt-out link.
2. **Preference Center Link**: Allowing users to select frequency (e.g. weekly digest instead of instant drop alerts).
3. **Physical Address**: Our registered office address (`[REGISTERED_OFFICE_ADDRESS]`) as required by anti-spam regulations in India.
