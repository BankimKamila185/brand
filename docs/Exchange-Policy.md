# Exchange Policy - The Outliers Studio

*Last Updated: July 20, 2026*

At **The Outliers Studio**, we understand that finding the perfect fit can sometimes be tricky—especially with our custom oversized streetwear cuts. This Exchange Policy outlines our guidelines for size and product exchanges.

---

## 1. Exchange Window
You can request an exchange within **7 days** of receiving your package. Exchange requests must be submitted online within 7 calendar days of delivery as logged by our logistics provider.

---

## 2. Conditions for Exchange
All items requested for exchange must meet the same strict quality guidelines as returns:
* **Brand New Condition**: The item must be unworn, unwashed, and free of any odor, perfume, pet hair, make-up, or stains.
* **Original Tags Attached**: All tags, wash care labels, and packaging must be intact.
* **Product Integrity**: The garment must not have been altered, tailored, or damaged in any way.

*Please note: Accessories (jewelry, socks, beanies, caps), innerwear, and clearance items are not eligible for exchange.*

---

## 3. How the Exchange Process Works
1. **Submit Request**: Visit the [Exchange Request Guide](Customer-Support.md#exchange-request-guide) or log in to your account. Select the order and click "Request Exchange". Specify the new size you want.
2. **Review**: Our customer service team will verify your request and check inventory for your requested size.
3. **Approval & Reverse Pickup**: If approved, we will book a reverse pickup for your original item.
4. **Fulfillment**: Once the original item is picked up (or returns to our warehouse and passes quality control), we will ship your replacement size.
5. **No Charges**: We offer **one (1) free exchange per order**—meaning we cover both the reverse pickup and redelivery shipping fees. Any subsequent exchanges on the same order will incur a flat shipping fee of **INR 149**.

---

## 4. Stock Availability Limitations
* **Subject to Stock**: Exchanges are subject to the availability of the requested size at our warehouse at the time your returned item is processed.
* **Out-of-Stock Scenario**: If the size you requested is out of stock:
  * We will contact you to offer a different design/color of equal value.
  * Alternatively, we can issue a full refund to your original payment source.

---

## 5. Exchanging for Different Products
* **Same Product**: Exchanges are only valid for size changes of the same product. 
* **Different Product**: If you want to exchange your item for a different product entirely, please request a standard return and refund, then place a new order for the desired item.
