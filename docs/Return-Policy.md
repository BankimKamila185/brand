# Return Policy - The Outliers Studio

*Last Updated: July 20, 2026*

We want you to love your streetwear fit. However, if you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help you get it sorted. This Return Policy outlines the eligibility criteria, process, and guidelines for returning items purchased from **The Outliers Studio**.

---

## 1. Return Window
We offer a **7-day return policy**. You must initiate a return request within **7 calendar days** from the date the order was delivered to your address as per the carrier tracking records. Requests received after 7 days will not be accepted.

---

## 2. Eligibility Criteria for Returns
To qualify for a return, the product must be sent back in its original condition. Items must meet the following strict conditions:

* **Unused & Unwashed**: The product must not have been worn (except for initial trial fit), washed, ironed, soiled, or altered.
* **Tags Intact**: All original brand tags, pricing tags, wash care labels, and security loops must remain attached to the garment in their original position.
* **Packaging**: The item must be returned in its original, undamaged packaging (including custom dust bags, boxes, or polybags).
* **Free of Damage**: The product must be free from any stains, perfume/laundry detergent scent, makeup marks, body odor, pet hair, or tears.

---

## 3. Non-Returnable Items
For hygiene and safety reasons, we do not accept returns on the following categories:
* **Innerwear & Socks**: Boxers, briefs, undershirts, and socks.
* **Accessories**: Jewelry, sunglasses, masks, beanies, caps, and stickers (unless delivered damaged).
* **Custom/Personalized Apparel**: Any item custom-printed or altered at your request.
* **Clearance/Duo Drops**: Items marked as "Clearance", "Final Sale", or "Warehouse Sale" on the product page.

---

## 4. How to Request a Return
1. **Submit Request**: Visit our [Return Request Guide](Customer-Support.md#return-request-guide) page, or log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to click "Request Return".
2. **Details & Images**: Upload at least **3 clear photographs** showing the front, back, and the attached tags of the product to help us verify its condition.
3. **Approval**: Our Quality Assurance (QA) team will review your request within 24–48 hours. If approved, we will schedule a reverse pickup.

---

## 5. Reverse Pickup Logistics
* **Serviceable Pincodes**: We provide free reverse pickup for most pincodes across India. Our logistics partner will pick up the package within 2–4 business days of approval.
* **Packing Instruction**: Please place the item in its original brand packaging, then seal it inside a secure outer courier bag. Hand over only the pre-approved item to the pickup agent.
* **Self-Shipping**: If your pincode is not serviceable for reverse pickup by our logistics partners, you must self-ship the item to our warehouse at your own expense.
  * **Warehouse Return Address**: `[WAREHOUSE_ADDRESS]`
  * **Self-Shipping Reimbursement**: We will credit **up to INR 100** to your account/bank as shipping reimbursement, provided you email a valid courier receipt to `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]`. We recommend using a reliable tracked courier (such as Speed Post or DTDC). We are not responsible for packages lost in transit during self-shipping.

---

## 6. Inspection & Final Decision
Once your return package arrives at our warehouse, our quality control team will inspect the item.
* **Approved Returns**: If the item passes quality control, we will process a refund according to our [Refund Policy](Refund-Policy.md).
* **Rejected Returns**: If the item fails quality control (e.g. shows signs of wear, washing, makeup, or missing tags), the return will be rejected. We will ship the product back to your address at your cost, and no refund will be issued.
