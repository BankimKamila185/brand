## 1. What Personal Data We Collect
We collect only the personal data necessary to provide you with our premium streetwear products and a seamless user experience.

### A. Information You Provide Directly
* **Identity Data**: Full name, gender, date of birth (optional, for age verification/birthday promotional programs).
* **Contact Data**: Billing address, delivery address, phone number, and email address.
* **Account Credentials**: Username, password, security options (when you create a customer account).
* **Communication Data**: Records of your communications with our customer support teams via email, phone, or WhatsApp.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
* **Device & Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, network provider, and device identifiers.
* **Usage & Analytics Data**: Pages visited, products viewed, time spent on pages, referral sources, and search queries (via Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, and Google Tag Manager).
* **Cookies & Tracking Data**: We use cookies to manage shopping carts, maintain sessions, and offer personalized content. (For details, please review our [Cookie Policy](Cookie-Policy.md)).

### C. Information from Third Parties
* **Payment Information**: We do not store or process your financial details (credit/debit cards, bank accounts, UPI credentials). Payments are processed securely via our payment gateway partner, **Razorpay**. Razorpay shares transaction status details (success/failure) and transaction IDs with us to confirm orders.

## 2. Grounds & Purposes for Data Processing
Under Section 6 of the DPDPA 2023, we process your personal data under the following lawful grounds:

### A. Explicit Consent
We process your data for marketing communications, personalized suggestions, and newsletters only when you have given us explicit, affirmative consent.

### B. Legitimate Use / Performance of Contract
We process your data to fulfill your order, which includes:
* Managing payments via Razorpay.
* Delivering packages to your address using shipping partners.
* Informing you about order updates via Resend (email), SMS, and WhatsApp.
* Handling refunds, exchanges, or customer service inquiries.

### C. Legal Obligation
We process and retain data to comply with Indian tax laws (such as GST compliance), financial regulations, or court orders.

## 3. Data Storage, Retention, and Location
* **Storage Infrastructure**: Your data is stored on secure databases (MongoDB/PostgreSQL) hosted within secure, encrypted cloud environments. Digital assets (like product images or user-uploaded media) are stored in **Cloudflare R2 Storage** (encrypted at rest).

* **Data Retention**: We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including satisfying any legal, accounting, tax, or reporting requirements. Once the purpose is complete or consent is withdrawn, we will permanently delete or anonymize your data, unless retention is mandated by law.

## 5. Your Rights as a Data Principal (DPDPA, 2023)
Under the DPDPA 2023, you possess the following rights regarding your personal data:

* **Right to Access**: You can request details of the personal data we hold about you and the processing activities we carry out.
* **Right to Correction & Erasure**: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data, or erase your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purpose it was collected.
* **Right to Withdraw Consent**: You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time (e.g., unsubscribing from marketing emails). The withdrawal of consent will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
* **Right of Grievance Redressal**: If you have any concerns regarding how your data is handled, you have the right to file a grievance with us or escalate it to the Data Protection Board of India.
* **Right to Nominate**: You can nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at `[GRIEVANCE_EMAIL]`.
