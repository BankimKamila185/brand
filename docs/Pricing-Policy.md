# Pricing Policy - The Outliers Studio

*Last Updated: July 20, 2026*

This Pricing Policy details the principles, rules, and structures governing product prices, taxes, discounts, and pricing errors on **The Outliers Studio** website (`[WEBSITE_URL]`).

---

## 1. Maximum Retail Price (MRP)
* All products offered on our Website are sold at their Maximum Retail Price (MRP) or below.
* The MRP includes the base manufacturing cost, design development, logistics, marketing, packaging, and all applicable taxes.
* Each physical product shipped is labeled with a price tag showing the MRP in compliance with the Legal Metrology Act, 2009 (India).

---

## 2. Goods and Services Tax (GST)
* In accordance with Indian tax laws, all prices displayed on our product pages are **inclusive of GST** (Goods and Services Tax).
* The applicable GST rate for apparel is calculated during checkout:
  * Clothing items priced under INR 1,000 carry a GST rate of **5%**.
  * Clothing items priced at or above INR 1,000 carry a GST rate of **12%**.
* A clear breakdown of CGST, SGST, and IGST paid will be detailed on your order confirmation email and the physical tax invoice included in your delivery.

---

## 3. Pricing Consistency & Alterations
* **Dynamic Updates**: We design and produce in limited quantities. We reserve the right to alter product prices, promotional discounts, and collections without prior notice.
* **No Price Protection**: The price you pay is the price active at the time you successfully complete your checkout. We do not offer price matching or refunds for price differences if an item you purchased goes on sale or is discounted after your order is confirmed.

---

## 4. Discount & Promotional Codes
* **Single Use**: Unless explicitly stated otherwise, promotional codes and coupons cannot be combined. Only one discount code can be applied per order.
* **Exclusions**: Certain high-end designer drops, limited editions, or charity items may be excluded from discount coupons. These exclusions will be highlighted on the product page.
* **Refund of Discounted Orders**: If you return an item purchased with a discount code, the refund will equal the exact net amount paid (after the discount) and not the original MRP. The discount code will not be re-issued or reactivated for future use.

---

## 5. Errors in Pricing
Despite our best efforts, technical errors in pricing may occasionally occur.
* **Cancellation Right**: In the event that a product is listed at an incorrect price due to a system glitch, typographical error, or database error, we reserve the right to cancel any orders placed for that product.
* **Resolution**: If your order is cancelled due to a pricing error:
  1. We will notify you immediately via email or phone.
  2. We will issue a full refund to your original payment source via Razorpay within 5–7 business days.
  3. You will be free to place a new order for the item at its correct updated price.
