# Email Marketing Consent & Subscriptions - The Outliers Studio

In compliance with the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)** and international anti-spam standards (like CASL/GDPR), all marketing email consents must be **explicit, opt-in, and unchecked by default**. We do not use pre-checked boxes or deceptive "dark pattern" wording.

This document contains production-ready copy for newsletters, checkouts, and account preferences.

---

## 1. Newsletter Signup Form (Homepage & Footer)

### UI Placement
A static form in the website footer or a pop-up modal.

### Copy
* **Input Placeholder**: `Enter your email address`
* **Submit Button**: `Join the Studio`
* **Consent Text (Directly below input)**:
  > "I would like to receive emails about new arrivals, exclusive offers, product launches, and updates. I understand that I can unsubscribe at any time. My data will be processed in accordance with the [Privacy Policy](Privacy-Policy.md)."

---

## 2. Checkout Marketing Consents (Optional Toggles)

### UI Placement
Under the shipping/contact details form during checkout. Both must be **unchecked by default**.

### A. General Promotional Consent
* **Toggle Label**:
  > "Receive newsletters and exclusive drop notifications."
* **Supporting Text (Small font)**:
  > "By checking this, you agree to receive promotional emails from The Outliers Studio regarding new streetwear collections, early access drops, and VIP sales. Unsubscribe at any time."

### B. Abandoned Cart Recovery Consent
* **Toggle Label**:
  > "Remind me about items left in my shopping cart."
* **Supporting Text (Small font)**:
  > "By checking this, you consent to receive reminder emails if you leave items in your shopping cart without completing checkout. We will send a maximum of two reminders."

### C. Personalized Recommendations Consent
* **Toggle Label**:
  > "Receive personalized product suggestions."
* **Supporting Text (Small font)**:
  > "By checking this, you consent to our analysis of your browsing history (e.g. products viewed) to send you tailored streetwear recommendations suited to your style."

---

## 3. Email Footer Requirements (Mandatory for Resend)
Every marketing email sent from our platform using **Resend** must include the following clear footer details:

* **Brand Details**:
  > "You are receiving this email because you opted in to receive updates from The Outliers Studio."
* **Unsubscribe Link**:
  > "No longer want to receive these emails? [Unsubscribe here](file:///unsubscribe-link) or [Manage Email Preferences](file:///preferences-link)."
* **Business Credentials**:
  > "The Outliers Studio | [BUSINESS_NAME] | [REGISTERED_OFFICE_ADDRESS] | Support: [SUPPORT_EMAIL]"

---

## 4. Preference Center Copy (Account Page)
* **Title**: "Manage Email Preferences"
* **Subtitle**: "Customize how and when we reach out to you."
* **Options**:
  * `[ ]` **Weekly Newsletter**: Weekly drops, streetwear styling blogs, and highlights.
  * `[ ]` **Exclusive Offers**: VIP sales, discount codes, and early access.
  * `[ ]` **Product Restocks**: Notifications when items on your wishlist return.
  * `[ ]` **Unsubscribe From All**: Immediately opt-out of all marketing communications. (Transactional emails regarding order updates will still be sent).
