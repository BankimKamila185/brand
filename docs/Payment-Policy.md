# Payment Policy - The Outliers Studio

*Last Updated: July 20, 2026*

This Payment Policy outlines the accepted payment modes, security protocols, transaction guidelines, and resolution procedures for billing-related matters on **The Outliers Studio** website.

---

## 1. Accepted Payment Methods
We partner with **Razorpay**, India’s leading secure payment gateway, to offer a comprehensive range of payment options. All prices are processed in Indian Rupees (INR).

### A. Online Prepaid Methods (Processed via Razorpay)
* **Credit and Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Diners Club, and Maestro.
* **Unified Payments Interface (UPI)**: Instant payment via Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, or any UPI ID.
* **Net Banking**: Supported for over 50+ major banks across India.
* **Digital Wallets**: Payments via Paytm, Mobikwik, PhonePe Wallet, and others.

### B. Cash on Delivery (COD)
* **Availability**: COD is available on selected pin codes for orders with values between **INR 999** and **INR 5,000**.
* **COD Convenience Fee**: A flat convenience fee of **INR 149** is applied to all COD orders.
* **Order Verification**: After placing a COD order, you will receive an automated WhatsApp confirmation message or a phone call to verify your shipping details. The order will be shipped only *after* successful verification.
* **Cash Only**: Our delivery agents cannot accept online payments (such as UPI/GPay) at the time of delivery unless the logistics provider specifically supports "Pay on Delivery" UPI QR scans. Please keep cash ready.

---

## 2. Transaction Security & Encryption
* **256-bit Encryption**: All transactional details are transmitted securely using industry-standard 256-bit Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption.
* **PCI-DSS Compliance**: Our payment processing is fully managed by Razorpay, which is certified as a **PCI-DSS Level 1 Service Provider** (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard). 
* **No Stored Details**: **The Outliers Studio** does not collect, access, or store your card numbers, CVVs, expiry dates, or internet banking passwords.

---

## 3. Failed Transactions & Auto-Refunds
Sometimes, a transaction might fail due to network drops, bank server issues, or incorrect verification codes.

### Q: Money was deducted from my account, but my order shows "Failed" or wasn't confirmed. What do I do?
* **Auto-Reversal**: If money has been deducted from your account for a failed order, the bank will automatically reverse the transaction.
* **Timeline**: The funds are usually returned to your source bank account within **3 to 5 business days** (for UPI/net banking) or **5 to 7 business days** (for cards) as per banking regulations.
* **Proof**: If the funds do not reflect in your account within this timeline, please email us at `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]` with:
  1. Your billing email and phone number.
  2. The date and approximate time of the transaction.
  3. The exact transaction reference number (UTR/RRN) or a screenshot of the debit notification.
  *We will coordinate with Razorpay to track the transaction and provide a resolution.*

## 4. Taxes & Duties
* **GST Included**: All product prices listed on our Website are inclusive of Goods and Services Tax (GST) as per the rates defined by the Government of India.
* **Tax Invoice**: A detailed Tax Invoice containing our GSTIN and a breakdown of SGST/CGST/IGST will be sent to your registered email upon dispatch and physical copy will be included inside the package.

## 5. Chargebacks & Disputes
If you notice any unauthorized transaction originating from our website, please report it to your bank and to our support team (`[SUPPORT_EMAIL]`) immediately. We work closely with payment processors and law enforcement to identify and resolve fraudulent transactions.
