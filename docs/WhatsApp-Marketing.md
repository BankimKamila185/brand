# WhatsApp Marketing & Notifications Consent - The Outliers Studio

WhatsApp is a primary communication tool in India. To respect customer boundaries and comply with Meta's Business Policies and the DPDPA 2023, we separate transactional updates from promotional marketing messages.

This document contains production-ready consent text and guidelines for WhatsApp communications.

---

## 1. Opt-In Consents at Checkout

### A. Combined Order Status & Promotional Consent
* **UI Placement**: Beneath the phone number entry field during guest checkouts or account creation. **Unchecked by default**.
* **Copy**:
  > "Get order updates, tracking alerts, and exclusive drop notifications on WhatsApp."
* **Disclaimer Copy (Small font)**:
  > "By checking this box, you authorize The Outliers Studio to send you transactional updates (order confirmation, shipping tracking, delivery updates) and promotional marketing messages (new launches, discount offers) via WhatsApp at the phone number provided. You can opt out at any time by replying 'STOP' to our WhatsApp chat or managing your preferences in your account settings."

### B. Split Consent (Strict Compliance Choice)
If we choose to split transactional alerts from marketing alerts to maximize conversion rates:
1. **Transactional Alerts (Pre-checked or Implied)**:
   > "Send my order confirmation and courier tracking alerts via WhatsApp."
2. **Promotional Alerts (Unchecked by default)**:
   > "Send me exclusive streetwear drop alerts and early access coupons on WhatsApp."

---

## 2. Message Category Classification

| Category | Typical Message Content | Trigger | Opt-out Route |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Transactional** | "Your order #[Order_ID] has been shipped! Track here: [Link]" | Order dispatch | Reply `STOP` or `UNSUBSCRIBE` |
| **Promotional** | "Hey [Name], our Winter Utility Hoodies just dropped! 🧥 Limited stocks. Grab yours: [Link]" | Campaign drop launch | Click the quick-reply `[Stop Messages]` button |
| **Abandoned Cart** | "We saved your cart! Your oversized t-shirt is waiting. Tap to checkout: [Link]" | Cart abandoned | Click the quick-reply `[Stop Messages]` button |

---

## 3. WhatsApp Message Footers (Meta Compliance)
To maintain our WhatsApp Business verified status, all promotional template messages must include an explicit quick-reply button for opting out.

### Standard Template Structure
* **Header**: Visual media (e.g. Lookbook image) or Bold Text
* **Body**: Promotional copy
* **Buttons**:
  1. `[Shop Now]` (Call to Action Link)
  2. `[Stop Messages]` (Opt-out Quick Reply - automatically tags user in our CRM as unsubscribed)
