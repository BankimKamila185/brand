# Customer Support & Care Guides - The Outliers Studio

This consolidated support manual contains sizing guides, wash care rules, and step-by-step procedures for managing orders, cancellations, returns, and exchanges on **The Outliers Studio**.

---

## 1. Size Guide (Oversized Streetwear Fit)
Our collections feature dropped shoulders, boxy cuts, and relaxed silhouettes. Use the table below to find your perfect fit. All measurements are in inches.

| Size | Chest (Width) | Length (Height) | Shoulder (Dropped) | Recommended Height |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **S** | 44" | 27.5" | 20.5" | 5'4" to 5'7" |
| **M** | 46" | 28.5" | 21.5" | 5'8" to 5'10" |
| **L** | 48" | 29.5" | 22.5" | 5'11" to 6'1" |
| **XL** | 50" | 30.5" | 23.5" | 6'2" and above |
| **XXL** | 52" | 31.5" | 24.5" | Tall & Boxy Fit |

* **Sizing Advice**: For the intended boxy, oversized streetwear look, order your standard size. If you prefer a normal, closer-to-body fit, size down one full size.

## 2. Wash Care Guide
Our premium heavy-weight cotton fabrics and graphic prints are designed to last. Follow these instructions to prevent shrinkage and print damage:

* **Wash Inside Out**: Protects the print graphic and embroidery from rubbing against other clothes.
* **Cold Machine Wash**: Wash in cold water (below 30°C) on a gentle cycle. Hot water shrinks heavy cotton.
* **Mild Detergent**: Avoid bleach, fabric softeners, or harsh stain removers.
* **Do Not Iron on Print**: Never run a hot iron directly over screen-printed graphics or embroidery. Iron the garment inside out on a low heat setting.
* **Air Dry in Shade**: Flat dry in shade. Avoid direct sunlight to prevent color fading. Do not tumble dry.

## 3. Order Tracking (Track Order)
Once shipped, tracking details are updated automatically:
1. Check your email or SMS for the tracking number and courier link.
2. Visit `[WEBSITE_URL]/track-order`.
3. Input your **Order ID** and **Email Address** or **Mobile Number** to view live shipping milestones.

---

## 4. Order Cancellation Guide
1. Log in to your profile on the website.
2. Go to **My Account** > **My Orders**.
3. If your order is within the **2-hour window** and has not been dispatched, a `[Cancel Order]` button will be active. Click it to cancel.
4. If you checked out as a guest, send a mail to `[SUPPORT_EMAIL]` with the subject line `URGENT: Cancel Order [Your Order ID]` within the 2-hour window.

---

## 5. Return Request Guide
1. Go to `[WEBSITE_URL]/returns` or click "Request Return" under "My Orders" in your dashboard.
2. Select the items you wish to return and choose the reason (e.g. damaged, wrong product).
3. Upload **3 high-quality photographs** showing the front, back, and price tags.
4. Submit the request. Once our team approves it (within 24–48 hours), you will receive a WhatsApp/email notification.
5. Pack the items in their original packaging, seal it, and wait for the courier pickup agent who will arrive in 2–4 business days.

---

## 6. Exchange Request Guide
1. Go to `[WEBSITE_URL]/exchanges` or select your order under account settings.
2. Choose the product to exchange, choose the reason ("Sizing/Fit adjustment"), and select your **new desired size** from the stock dropdown.
3. Submit the request.
4. Upon approval, we will schedule a reverse pickup.
5. **Fulfillment**: Once the pickup agent confirms collection, we will ship the new size from our warehouse.
